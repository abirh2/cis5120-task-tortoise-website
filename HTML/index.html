CBT-oriented Methods of Help

TaskTortoise utilizes methods of helping those with ADHD through Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) solutions

Implicit Social Accountability

Our app provides a platform for individuals, especially those with ADHD, to support and hold each other accountable. By connecting users, our app fosters a supportive environment where individuals can set goals, track progress, and receive encouragement from peers. This accountability loop not only helps users stay focused and motivated but also enhances their sense of community and belonging, which are vital for managing ADHD symptoms effectively.

Task Breakdowns

By breaking tasks into bite-sized chunks, users can approach their goals with less overwhelm and greater clarity. This feature is especially beneficial for individuals with ADHD, as it facilitates focus and reduces procrastination by providing clear, actionable steps. Additionally, users can track their progress on each subtask, leading to a sense of accomplishment and motivation to continue moving forward.

Building Structure

Users can organize their tasks, set deadlines, and receive reminders, providing a framework for productivity. Additionally, our app enables users to monitor progress on individual tasks and view their overall accomplishments over time, offering a comprehensive perspective on their productivity. This structured approach helps users stay on track, manage their time efficiently, and maintain a sense of control over their responsibilities, ultimately enhancing their ability to stay focused and achieve their goals.

Progress Visualization

Our app offers interactive charts and graphs to visually track your progress over time, providing a clear snapshot of your accomplishments and motivating you to keep going.

Automatic Task Breakdowns

Utilizing advanced LLMs/AI technology, our app automatically breaks down tasks into smaller, more manageable steps, making it easier for you to approach your goals with clarity and focus.

AI Buddy for Accountability

Engage in meaningful conversations with our AI buddy, who provides encouragement, support, and gentle reminders to help you stay on track and accountable towards your goals.

Task Sharing with Friends

Share your tasks with friends and loved ones, fostering a supportive environment where you can cheer each other on, collaborate, and celebrate achievements together.

Deadline Tracking

Set deadlines for your tasks and effortlessly track your progress towards meeting them, ensuring you stay organized and productive while managing your time effectively.

Personalized Features

Our app offers a range of personalized features tailored to your specific needs and preferences, empowering you to customize your experience and optimize your productivity journey.
